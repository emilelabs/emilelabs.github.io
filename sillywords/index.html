Silly Words

Read & Play — sight-word reading for ages 6-8. Read real words, spot the silly one, earn stars.

  • The silly twist: every round hides one made-up word — catching it keeps young readers honest (and giggling)
  • Fully offline: no ads, no tracking, no data collection — everything stays on your device
  • For parents: words known, tricky words, progress against reading standards, adjustable difficulty
