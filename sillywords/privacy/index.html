Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Emile Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Silly Words mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our approach to privacy: Silly Words does not collect any data.

In short: Silly Words is fully offline. It collects no personal information, uses no analytics, shows no ads, contains no tracking, and never transmits anything over the internet. Everything your child does in the App stays on your device.

1. Children's Privacy

Silly Words is designed for children aged 6-8. We take children's privacy very seriously.

  • We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and applicable international children's privacy regulations.
  • We do not collect personal information from anyone — child or adult.
  • Child profiles in the App use only a first name (or nickname) and an emoji avatar, which are stored locally on the device and never leave it.

2. Information We Collect

None. The App has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs. It makes no network requests.

Information Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

  • Profile names and emoji avatars
  • Reading progress: stars, streaks, badges, session history, and the spaced-repetition schedule of practised words
  • App settings (sounds, haptics, session length, daily reminder time)

This information never leaves your device. We cannot see it, and neither can anyone else.

3. Subscriptions

Premium and Family subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not receive or store your name, payment details, or Apple ID. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Notifications

If you enable the optional daily practice reminder, the notification is scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to us or to any third party.

5. Data Storage and Security

All App data is stored in the App's private storage area on your device, protected by iOS's standard sandboxing and device security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Progress can be reset per child from the "For parents" dashboard inside the App.
  • Deleting a profile removes that child's progress data from the device.
  • Deleting the App removes all App data from the device.

7. Parental Controls

Purchases, external links, and the parents' dashboard are protected by a parental gate. Purchases additionally require Apple ID authentication managed by Apple (including Ask to Buy where enabled).

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party services, SDKs, analytics, or advertising. The only external party involved in your use of the App is Apple, for App Store distribution and subscription billing.

9. Changes to This Policy

If a future version of the App ever changes this approach, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy label before release.
