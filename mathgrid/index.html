Auto Adaptive Difficulty

Battle a Friend

Race a friend in real time — challenge an online friend with a tap, or share a 6-digit room code. Both play the identical grid, fastest time wins. Only a first name and avatar are shared to run the match.

Friends & Presence

Add a friend with a simple code, see who's online with a live dot, and compare weekly and all-time stars. Friendships form only when both sides accept.

Simple as 1, 2, 3

How the 100-Square Works

The drill that generations of Japanese schoolchildren grew up with — three steps to math fluency.

1
🎯

Pick an Operation & Grid

Choose + − × or ÷ and a grid size — or let 🪄 Auto pick the perfect level automatically.

2
✏️

Fill Every Cell

Numbers run across the top and down the side. Your child combines them with the corner operation and fills the whole grid.

3
🏆

Beat the Clock, Level Up

Earn stars, build streaks and watch the heatmap fill in. Two strong rounds and the difficulty steps up on its own.

Made for Parents Too

Truly Private. Made for Families.

Built with care for young learners — and built to keep your family's data private.

🔒

Private by Design

No accounts. No ads. No third-party analytics. No tracking. Your child's progress stays on the device. The only data ever shared is for the optional online features — a first name and avatar to run an Online Battle, and, if your child adds friends, a first name, avatar and star counts shared only with friends you've both accepted. Everything travels over an encrypted connection. COPPA-conscious by design.

🛡️

Parental Gates Everywhere

An adult challenge protects the parent dashboard, all purchases, external links and sharing — kids stay safely inside the game.

📊

A Real Parent Dashboard

Set per-operation levels, review accuracy charts, see the list of tricky facts your child is working on, and browse the session log.

🌍

Speaks Your Language

English UI localised into Japanese, Chinese (Simplified), Korean, Spanish, Portuguese (BR), French and German.

🌱

Encouragement, Not Pressure

Auto difficulty adjusts down gently and never frames it as failure. Stars and streaks reward showing up, not just speed.
