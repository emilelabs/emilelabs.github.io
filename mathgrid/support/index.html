Common Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Open Math Grid > Settings > Premium > Restore Purchases

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Math Grid > Cancel

How do I delete my data?

You can remove individual profiles in the Profiles screen, or uninstall the app to delete all data. All data is stored locally on your device.

What is Math Grid?

Math Grid brings the classic hyakumasu (100-square) calculation method to iPad — timed grids that build fast, confident mental arithmetic.
