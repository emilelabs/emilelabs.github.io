2. Description of Service

Math Grid is an educational App designed for children aged 5-7 and up that builds math fluency through the Japanese 100-square calculation drill (hyakumasu keisan). The App offers:

  • Free features: 3×3 and 4×4 grids, all four operations (+ − × ÷), Auto adaptive difficulty, battles (local and online), up to 3 player profiles
  • Premium features: All grid sizes including the full 100-square (10×10), manual level picking, premium avatars, and progress backup & restore (available via subscription)
  • Family features: All Premium features for up to 6 player profiles, with Family Sharing

Core play requires no account and no registration. The single-player and local game modes work fully offline; the optional Online Battle multiplayer feature requires an internet connection (see Section 7).
