Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Emile Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Math Grid mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

In short: Math Grid is 100% offline. We collect no data. Nothing ever leaves your device.

1. Children's Privacy

Math Grid is designed for children aged 5-7 and up and is offered in the App Store Kids Category. We take children's privacy very seriously.

  • We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and applicable international children's privacy regulations.
  • We do not collect personal information from anyone — children or adults. The App has no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no network features.
  • Player profiles in the App use only a first name, avatar, and colour, which are stored locally on the device.
  • Parental gates (an adult challenge) protect the parent dashboard, all purchases, external links, and sharing.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

All App data is stored locally on your device only, in local JSON files:

  • Profile data: First name, avatar, and colour for each player profile
  • Progress data: Scores, stars, streaks, levels, practice heatmap, tricky-facts list, and session logs
  • Battle records: Results of offline battles, including battle codes you have entered
  • Settings: App preferences and subscription status

This data is never transmitted anywhere. We have no servers and the App makes no network connections for gameplay.

3. Battle Mode Is Offline

Math Grid's Battle mode does not use the internet. A battle code is simply a 12-character secret code that one player shares with another (for example, spoken aloud or written down) and the other player types in. The code itself describes the battle grid — no data is sent or received over any network, and there is no online matchmaking or multiplayer service.

4. Information Processed by Third Parties

  • Apple (StoreKit): When you make a purchase, Apple processes the transaction. We do not receive or store your payment information. Purchases are protected by a parental gate within the App.

We use no other third-party services — no Firebase, no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no tracking of any kind.

5. How We Use Information

Because all data stays on your device, the only "use" of information is by the App itself, locally, to:

  • Provide core App functionality (profiles, grids, scores, leaderboards, heatmap, smart practice)
  • Power the parent dashboard (per-operation levels, accuracy charts, tricky facts, session log)
  • Unlock features according to your subscription status (verified via Apple)

We do not:

  • Collect, sell, rent, or share any personal information
  • Use data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Track users across apps or websites
  • Use analytics or tracking SDKs

6. Subscriptions

Math Grid offers auto-renewable subscriptions managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information.

  • Premium Monthly: $2.99/month
  • Premium Annual: $24.99/year
  • Family Annual: $29.99/year (supports Family Sharing, up to 6 player profiles)

Prices may vary by region based on Apple's pricing tiers. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage subscriptions in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

7. Data Storage and Security

  • All profile, progress, and battle data is stored locally on your device in local JSON files using standard iOS storage mechanisms.
  • We do not operate any external servers or databases. There is nothing for us to secure remotely because no data ever leaves your device.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All data is local: Deleting a profile within the App removes that profile's data.
  • To delete all data, simply uninstall the App from your device. Because we hold no copies, deletion is immediate and complete.

9. Parental Controls

  • The parent dashboard, all purchases, external links, and sharing are protected by a parental gate (an adult challenge)
  • Parents can manage player profiles and per-operation difficulty levels within the App
  • Subscription purchases additionally require Apple ID authentication (Apple's parental controls apply)
  • No account creation or email registration is required — or even possible
