Common Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Open DotsCard > Settings > Upgrade to Premium > Restore Purchases

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > DotsCard > Cancel

How do I delete my data?

You can remove individual profiles in the Profiles screen, or uninstall the app to delete all data. All data is stored locally on your device.

What ages is DotsCard for?

DotsCard is designed for children aged 5-9, building number sense through dot-pattern recognition (subitising).
